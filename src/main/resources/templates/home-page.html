Our Purpose

Your vote matters! Traditional voting systems often come with barriers that
can discourage participation, making it harder for people to have their voices heard.

Polling stations

Voters must travel to designated polling locations, which can be inconvenient and discourage participation

Lack of transparency

Voters never receive confirmation that their ballot was counted, raising concerns about trust in the system

Lost or tampered ballots

Reports of missing or altered ballots make many voters question the fairness and security of elections

Counting errors

Vote counting is done by election officials, and human error can lead to miscounts that impact election results

Our purpose is to solve the issues & uncertainty of traditional
voting systems by removing these obstacles.

Our Goals

Secure

Designed with multi-layered security to prevent unauthorized access.

We've implemented countless security measures to protect sensitive data while defending against potential attackers.

Trustable

Building confidence through transparency and verifiability.

We've eliminated the need for a central authority. Rather than solely relying on us, our clients can trust the formally proven methods in place that ensure the integrity of the process while also protecting their anonymity.

Transparent

Our Solutions

Double-Blind Registration

The double-blind registration process has been used to protect voters anonymity not only from external parties but from the system itself

3 Ballot System

The 3-ballot voting system provides integrity and prevents practices like vote selling

Prêt à Voter

Prêt à voter eliminates candidate bias and guarantees that election results cannot be tallied before the election ends

Ring Signatures

Ring signatures ensure that only eligible voters can cast their votes maintaining security and authenticity

Distributed Ledger

The distributed ledger solution provides transparency and ensures the immutability of ballot records

Getting Started

Step 1: Register

Enter your registration key distributed by an external party.

Step 2: Set credentials

Provide a username that is not self-identifying and a strong password that follows our password policy.

Step 3: Enter your signing key

Enter your signing key stored on your device.

Step 4: Select your candidate & the ballot you would like to keep

Following the 3-ballot voting system, select your preferred candidate.

Step 5: Cast your ballots & save a copy of your receipt

Either email or download a PDF copy of your receipt.

Step 6 (Optional): Find your ballot on the ledger

Using the ballot ID on your receipt query the ledger to ensure integrity.
